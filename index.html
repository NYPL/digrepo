--- layout: default title: Home exclude: True --- ## NYPL Digital Repository Program New York Public Library's Digital Repository program is responsible for the ingest and maintenance of NYPL's digitized and born-digital research collections. It began in 2022 as part of the [Digital Preservation](https://nypl.github.io/digpres/) department within Preservaiton and Collections Processing division of the NYPL Research Libraries. This site documents: * the policies of the New York Public Library Digital Repository Program * the usage of Preservica as the Library's digital repository * the usage of the Library's image repository
